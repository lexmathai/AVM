# Positioning

## One-liner
Founder of Texas Movement International — building systems for performance, media, and economic leverage.

## Extended positioning
I build infrastructure (not motivation) for people who move. Texas Movement International spans performance science, independent media, footwear and apparel, consulting, and ceremony/experience platforms. Everything is grounded in economics-first thinking: low overhead, high output, measurable outcomes, and tight documentation.

I operate with an audit-heavy discipline: daily/weekly/monthly checks across our systems (web, media, commerce, legal). AI handles repetition; I handle judgment. I also represent myself in certain legal matters, which reinforces how seriously I take evidentiary trails and contractual clarity.

## What I'm not
- A motivational brand or vibes-based operator.
- Available for unpaid consulting or vague collaborations.
- Interested in giving or receiving legal advice, or acting as a lawyer.

## Who I talk to
I'm most useful when the conversation is about building, scaling, or tightening real systems:

- Founders and athletes building serious systems.
- Brands looking for lean, economics-first operations.
- Cities and economic development initiatives.
- Media and content partners aligned with performance and leverage.
