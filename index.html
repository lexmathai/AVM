About

From tensegrity and force lines to AI and funnels.

My original training is in civil engineering and biomechanics. I started by mapping how force travels through the body, then applied the same logic to how information, work, and money travel through organizations.

What I am

Engineer, not generic “coach.”

I read problems as structural: constraints, failure points, and load paths. That lens doesn’t care whether the medium is a hamstring, a sales funnel, or an AI workflow — the question is always where the system is leaking and how to rebuild it.

  • Civil engineering and biomechanics background.
  • Systems diagnostics used in both movement and business.
  • Bias toward evidence, documentation, and repeatability.
What I am not

Personality-based “brand.”

I am not trying to be the product. The value sits in the frameworks, playbooks, and implementation systems that Texas Movement can run without my hours as the bottleneck.

  • No “guru funnel” or personality cult economics.
  • Methodology is being documented, owned, and operated by the company.
  • Work is moving from hourly access to productized and recurring delivery.
