About

I'm the founder and president of Texas Movement International, a systems company building infrastructure for performance, media, footwear, and city-scale economic development. We operate across multiple verticals—media production, apparel and footwear engineering, AI-driven consulting, and ceremony technology—all anchored in the thesis that economic leverage comes from systems, not motivation.

I work with an audit-heavy operating style: daily and weekly checks across our infrastructure (web, media, commerce, legal). AI handles repetition; I handle judgment. Everything we build is grounded in evidence, documentation, and measurable outcomes. I also represent myself legally, which reinforces how seriously I take evidentiary trails and contractual clarity.

The work spans founders, athletes, and cities looking for lean systems that compound. If you're building something serious and want to talk infrastructure, let's go.
